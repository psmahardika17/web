Everyday Life




Everyday Life is the eighth studio album by British rock band Coldplay, released on 22 November 2019. It is a double album (released as a single CD), with the first half titled Sunrise and the other Sunset.

COLDPLAY



Coldplay are a British rock band formed in London in 1996.Vocalist and pianist Chris Martin, guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, and drummer Will Champion met at University College London and began playing music together from 1996 to 1998, first calling themselves Pectoralz and then Starfish before finally changing their name to Coldplay.
